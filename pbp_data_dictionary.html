About This Data

This comprehensive data dictionary documents all columns available in the NFL play-by-play dataset from nflverse/nflreadpy. The data includes detailed play-level information from every NFL game, with advanced analytics including Expected Points Added (EPA), Win Probability Added (WPA), and Next Gen Stats metrics.

Key Metrics Explained

  • EPA (Expected Points Added): Measure of a play's impact on the score. Difference between expected points before and after the play.
  • WPA (Win Probability Added): Change in win probability resulting from a play.
  • CPOE (Completion % Over Expected): How much better/worse a QB's completion rate is compared to expectation based on throw difficulty.
  • xYAC (Expected Yards After Catch): Predicted yards after catch based on where the ball was caught and defensive positioning.
